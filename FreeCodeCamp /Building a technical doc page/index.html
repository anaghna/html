Computer Engineering

Computer engineering is a discipline that integrates electrical engineering and computer science to develop computer systems. It involves both hardware (physical devices) and software (programming and system design).

Computer engineers work on the design and development of microprocessors, embedded systems, computer networks, and more. Their work is foundational in building modern computing devices and systems.

It includes areas such as operating systems, digital logic, networking, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence.
